In recent years, a brighter light has began to shine on companies' social responsibilty efforts. As the world's population increases and the conservation of natural resources become more important over time, long-term investors have a tremendous opportunity to invest in companies that strive to be leaders in sustainable business practices as well as align with their own personal values.



The chart below is a visualization of the ESG scores published by MSCI (Morgan Stanley Capital International) for the companies in The DOW 30. Each company is scored and then ranked amongst their industry peers, allowing for even comparison of effort between companies with similar business processes. The Overall ESG Score is based on the individual performance of a company in three measurable categories; Environmental, Social, and Governance.
